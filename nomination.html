Narendra Modi

Narendra Damodardas Modi is an Indian politician serving as the 14th and current Prime Minister of India since 2014. He was the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014 and is the Member of Parliament for Varanasi.
  • Born: September 17, 1950 (age 70), Vadnagar
  • Spouse: Jashodaben Modi
  • Education: Gujarat University (1983), University of Delhi (1978)
  • Parents: Heeraben Modi, Damodardas Mulchand Modi
  • Awards: CNN-IBN Indian of the Year
  • Party: Bharatiya Janata Party(BJP)

Rahul Gandhi

Rahul Gandhi is an Indian politician and a member of the Indian Parliament, representing the constituency of Wayanad, Kerala in the 17th Lok Sabha.
  • Born: June 19, 1970 (age 50), New Delhi
  • Parents: Sonia Gandhi, Rajiv Gandhi
  • Siblings: Priyanka Gandhi
  • Education: Trinity College, Cambridge (1994–1995)
  • Awards: CNN-IBN Indian of the Year in Politics
  • He served as the President of the Indian National Congress from 16 December 2017 to 3 July 2019.
  • Party: Indian National Congress (INC)

Arvind Kejriwal

Arvind Kejriwal is an Indian politician and a former bureaucrat who is the current and 7th Chief Minister of Delhi
  • Born: 16 August 1968 (age 52 years), Siwani
  • Education: Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (1985–1989)
  • Spouse: Sunita Kejriwal (m. 1994)
  • Awards: Ramon Magsaysay Award, CNN-IBN Indian of the Year in Politics
  • Party: Aam Aadmi Party(AAP)

Mamata Banerjee

Mamata Banerjee is an Indian politician who is serving as the 8th and current Chief Minister of West Bengal
  • Born: 5 January 1955 (age 65 years), Kolkata
  • Education: University of Calcutta (BA, MA, B.Ed, LLB)
  • Party: All India Trinamool Congress(TMC)
